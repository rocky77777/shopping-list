Billy's Shopping List

Type in the box and then press the 'add' button to register everything you're going to buy, but forget about and leave in the fridge until it goes bad because you go out to restaurants every night instead of cooking.
After you buy the item, click the 'Bought it' button. You'll still be able to see the item, though. If you want to delete it off the list, click the 'CLEAR ALL PURCHASED ITEMS' button.
You're supposed to enter an item into the box before you press the 'add' button so that it gets added to your list of stuff you're not going to eat.
